Guidelines, Values & Policy

Our Story

Founded in 2026, Fashion Hub was born from a desire to bridge the gap between traditional artisanal craftsmanship and the fluid requirements of modern life. We believe that what we wear should be a reflection of our values: quality, longevity, and conscious intent.

Our "Digital Atelier" model allows us to curate collections from master craftsmen around the globe, ensuring that every stitch and seam meets the highest standards of the sartorial arts while remaining accessible to the discerning individual.

Shipping & Returns

Complimentary Shipping

We offer global standard shipping on all orders over ₹500. For orders below this threshold, a flat rate is calculated based on destination at checkout.

Returns Policy

Items may be returned within 14 days of receipt, provided they are in original condition with all tags attached. Please note that personalized or final sale items are not eligible for return.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is of utmost importance to us. We collect only the information necessary to process your orders and enhance your experience at the Digital Atelier. We never share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

For a detailed breakdown of how we manage cookies and personal data, please contact our Data Protection Officer at privacy@fashionhub.com.
